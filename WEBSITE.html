About Us

History

The history of Britesparks roots from its establishment in year 2000 as a progressive preschool center. With the goal of guiding its students to explore their capabilities and discover new things in their most effective method, BIS has kept on innovating both its teaching approach and its learning environment to bring the best out of every student

Curriculum

Britesparks International School follows a curriculum based on the international standards that challenges its students with ambitious but achievable goals. Topics within subject areas are carefully articulated to ensure that there is a clear progression as students move to a higher level and to provide a richer learning experience to all its students

Mission

To provide exceptional and accessible international educational services with a school experience that will allow students to reach their fullest potential, form exemplary character, and to lay a foundation for a successful, productive and satisfying life. Our dream is to see our students in the future be achieving their dreams, and becoming leaders in society.
